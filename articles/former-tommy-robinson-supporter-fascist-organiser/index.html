Former Tommy Robinson Supporter is Fascist Organiser

by Red Flare  •  02.12.2020

We can reveal that fascist party Patriotic Alternative (PA)’s regional organiser for the East of England is Chris Mitchell, also known as Chris Patriotic Talk.

Mitchell is the owner of Patriotic Talk, formerly TR Talk: once the largest Tommy Robinson supporter group chat on Telegram. It is now one of the largest British ethnonationalist Telegram groups with over 2,600 subscribers.

Extreme racism, anti-Semitism, Holocaust denial and open veneration of Hitler are now regular features of Mitchell’s Telegram group, in which he describes himself as a ‘Nazi Buddhist’.

In 2019, Mitchell was radicalised by Mark Collett, ditching his civic nationalist politics for a more overtly racist ethnonationalism.

Mitchell is also a key figure behind the groyping phenomenon in the UK, where neo-Nazis call talk radio shows and flood YouTube livestreams with comments pushing their worldview.

As PA’s RO Mitchell organised a hike in Norfolk where a ‘White Lives Matter’ banner was unveiled. Mitchell has also published a photograph of a member leafleting while wearing a British Union of Fascists badge.
